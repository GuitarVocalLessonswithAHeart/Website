About Will

Will began playing guitar at the age of 15 after watching his friends play in a band. It looked so fun that Will took the $200.00 he had been saving up, got a ride to the local music store, and bought himself an electric guitar and an amp. Instantly he was hooked on practicing and in a short time could play many of his favorite songs.

30 years later, after obtaining multiple college degrees in music/guitar, performing in numerous bands in all music styles, and literally teaching thousands of students, Will's love for learning, playing, and teaching music continues to this day.

Services Offered

  • Virtual as well as in-person guitar/vocal lessons for all levels and ages
  • Music theory, note reading, tablature reading, ear training, rhythm counting, pick and fingerstyle technique, performance, and more
  • Instruction in all music styles
  • Acoustic, electric, and bass guitar with or without voice

Annual Music Recital

Will hosts an in-person as well as a virtual music recital at least twice a year for students who wish to showcase their talents in front of friends and family.
